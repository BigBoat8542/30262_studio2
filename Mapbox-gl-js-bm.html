This Map contains all of the Polling places and Political Wards in the City of Philadelphia, and can be used as a resource for eager voters as local elections come up this spring. The daata was collected from opendataaphilly.com and uses the colors of the city of Philadelphia. To define the colors, I googled hex colors and found some that resembled closely to the city's colors on the logo. I kept a white background for simplicity, as this map serves the purpose of informing voters where to vote and wards rather than many other major details.
